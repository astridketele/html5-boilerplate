    About Trax
  • Traveling is not always fun and dandy. Every traveler faces some challenges. How to spend you time efficiently? What are the must see sights and attractions? What if you feel lonely?

    Trax offers a solution for all of these challenges. It enhances your travel experience by showing top rated sights and showing all travelers that are near to you. With this app you are able to talk to other travelers, ask for recommendations and check the ratings of all the sights that are near you.
